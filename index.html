STAGE 01

Surface Cleaning & Metal Degreasing

Before electroplating, the metal substrate must be completely free of grease, oils, rust, and soils to ensure perfect metal-to-metal bonding. Failure to clean results in peeling, pitting, and bubbles in the plating layer.

Essential Allied Products used:

  • Metal Cleaner Solutions (Formulated to break down heavy industrial greases)
  • Dry Acids & Soda Ash (Used to adjust pH and neutralize surfaces)
  • Water-based cleaning chemicals (Eco-friendly precision degreasers)
STAGE 02

Acid Pickling & Scale Removal

This stage removes oxides, rust, scale, and tarnish from the cleaned metal substrate. By immersing the part in an acid bath, the metal is micro-etched, leaving a clean, highly receptive surface ready for atomic metal bonding.

Essential Allied Products used:

  • Hydrochloric Acid (H.C.L) (High concentration for rust etching)
  • Sulfuric Acid (Suitable for pickling steel and copper alloys)
  • Rust Revenge Soak (Selective water-based rust removing liquid that works safely on steel)
STAGE 03

Electroplating Bath (Deposition)

The cleaned object is immersed in the plating solution where an electric current transfers metal ions from anodes (metal sheets) onto the object. This deposits a thin, uniform protective and shiny metal layer.

Essential Allied Products used:

  • INCO Europe Anode Nickel Sheets (Ultra-pure nickel for corrosion resistance)
  • Zinc Metal & Zinc Cyanide/Chloride (For economical rust protection)
  • Brighteners & Levellers (Nickel Brightener 1001/1002, Zinc Brightener for mirror-like luster)
STAGE 04

Continuous Solution Filtration

To maintain high anti-corrosion properties and aesthetic shine, the electroplating bath must be filtered continuously. Micro-impurities, carbon particles, and dust are removed to prevent rough coatings or surface defects.

Essential Allied Products used:

  • Activated Double-Tower Filters (Centrifugal filter systems)
  • Filter Cartridges & Filter Cloth (Precision micron filtration bags)
  • Holland Carbon Powder (Bath purification and organic chemical adsorption)
STAGE 05

Surface Polishing & Final Finish

The plated parts undergo final buffing and mechanical polishing to eliminate minor scratches, enhance gloss, and seal the metallic surface. This elevates the finish into a premium grade commercially ready for distribution.

Essential Allied Products used:

  • Abrasive Belts (60 to 400 grit) (Grinding and pre-smoothing)
  • Buffs & White Special Buffs (Polishing wheels for mirror finish)
  • Falcon White & Leather SAN Polishes (High-grade wax bar compounds)
